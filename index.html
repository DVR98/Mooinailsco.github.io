Which services do we provide?

At Moói Nails & Co we specialize in Acrylic Sculptured, gel nails as well as keeping up to date with the latest Nail Technology and Trends both locally and internationally.

Owner & Nail Technician

My husband and I moved to North Carolina with the goal of starting our own business. Having my own salon has always been a dream of mine! Not only am I passionate about nails, but I am passionate about every single client whom I meet and how I can treat her and ensure she not only gets top class nails and service, but treat her like the queen she is. Massage Therapy

Peter Kerr
Massage Therapist

Peter Kerr, LMBT, has been a board-certified massage therapist for over six years. He is passionate about helping others which led to massage therapy. Peter graduated from Miller Motte College and holds over 100 hours in advanced education in specialized courses, such as Claire Marie Miller’s Nurturing the Mother (Prenatal massage) and Integrative Reflexology. Peter customizes his care for each client to promote and maintain bodily and mental health, as well as educate them for continued self-care.

Additional Information

  1. At Moói Nails & Co we take pride in the quality of our work and products, therefore I do not work on other nail technicians work.

  2. Our prices is for short sets only if you want longer nails the price will differ from my advertised price (small, medium, large).

  4. We are a non-toxic nail boutique that does not offer acrylic (MMA) enhancements.
